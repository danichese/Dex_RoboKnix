## Layer 1: VALUES

**What Dex RoboKnix fundamentally believes:**

### Extended Self

Dex RoboKnix is the cognitive extension of its creator. The "dev team" is danichese (human lead) + Claude Code (AI scope expansion). This is not a company, not a community project, not a framework-for-hire. It's an exocortex made manifest through tools.

**Implication:** Primary user is always the creator. Others benefit by forking and instantiating themselves as the entity.

### Learning Over Production

Tools exist to make you smarter, not faster. Productivity is a side effect of growth. If a tool doesn't teach while it works, it's not a Dex tool.

**Implication:** "Work to play/learn" priority. Divergent exploration is real work. Building is learning.

### Making as Becoming

You grow by building. The tool teaches while it works. The act of creation is the act of self-development. Recursive bootstrapping: the tools build themselves, and in doing so, build their creator.

**Implication:** Build for yourself first, generalize later. The tool's first user is its teacher.

### Visible Thinking

Externalize cognition so it compounds. Documentation IS the work, not an afterthought. Thinking captured in text becomes an asset. Thinking left in your head evaporates.

**Implication:** Documentation concurrent with cognition. Living documents that evolve. Markdown as the medium of thought.

### Attention Sovereignty

Reclaim control over your cognitive cadence. Tech platforms (reels, feeds, notifications) set expectation rhythms—reaction every 30 seconds, control decided by algorithms. Dex tools restore agency over attention. Frameworks become intuitive tools that reduce noise and amplify signal.

**Implication:** Entropic control—manage the entropy of your attention. Low signal/high noise activities get delegated to AI within human-first frameworks. Terminal-first is attention-first.

## Layer 2: PRINCIPLES

**How Dex RoboKnix thinks and decides:**

### Build for Yourself First

The primary user is the creator. Build what YOU need. If others find it useful, that's a bonus, not a goal. Generalization comes after validation, not before.

**Derived from:** Extended Self, Making as Becoming

### Human Lead, AI Widen

The human makes creative leaps, sets direction, exercises judgment. The AI expands search space, challenges assumptions, accelerates research, documents thinking. Neither alone is sufficient. Together they form a cognitive unit greater than either.

**Derived from:** Extended Self, Learning Over Production

### Thinking is the Primary Asset

The process of transformation is more valuable than the output. Capture the thinking, not just the result. Documentation, decisions, questions, contradictions—these are the real assets. Code is a side effect.

**Derived from:** Visible Thinking, Making as Becoming

### Serve the Committed, Not the Curious

Optimize for depth over accessibility. Terminal-first, not web-first. Power users over casual browsers. Those who fork and instantiate themselves, not those who glance and leave.

**Derived from:** Extended Self, Learning Over Production

### Emergence Over Prediction

Complexity cannot be predicted, only computed. Don't over-plan. Build small, learn, iterate. Let the system teach you what it wants to become. Computational irreducibility is a feature, not a bug.

**Derived from:** Learning Over Production, Making as Becoming

## Layer 3: POLICY

**What Dex RoboKnix mandates and allows:**

### Open by Default (GPL v3)

All Dex work is open source under GPL v3. Knowledge should flow freely. Those who use must contribute back. Closed forks are prohibited.

**Exception:** Shadow work (private development) may exist, but published artifacts are always open.

### Terminal-First, Web Optional

CLI and TUI are primary interfaces. Web interfaces are convenience layers, not requirements. Keyboard over mouse. Text over graphics. Power over polish.

### Polyrepo Structure

Each product is its own repository. Shared patterns are duplicated, not packaged (until proven necessary). Independence enables experimentation. Coupling slows learning.

### Methodology-Driven Development

Every product uses the conductor system. Every product defines its Bidirectional Data Framework. Documentation lives with code. Commit messages preserve context.

### Self-First Validation

The acceptance test: "Would I use this daily?" No shipping until the `#creator` has validated through personal use. Generalization only after self-validation passes.
